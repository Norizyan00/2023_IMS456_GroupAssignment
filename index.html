STORY OF TUDONGLAWA



Assalamualaikum warahmatullahi wabarakatuh,

TudongLawa is an existing business of a young entrepreneur. TudongLawa was owned by our group member, Nor Izyan Binti Zolkeflee and her partners, Nur Amirah Fatini Mohd Ghazali, Nisrin Abd Jamil and Nabilah Abdullah became a dropship to TudongLawa and earned profit and experience through this. The company is based in Pengkalan Chepa, Kelantan. We also took the opportunity by taking some stock to sell to UiTM students, especially on programs organized by UiTM such as HOMSIS, Finceptive Fiesta and so on. This is because students, especially women, are very fond of styling shawls so this gives us a good opportunity to sell shawls from our store. It is affordable and reasonable for students and individuals or families to buy this product as there are a wide range of prices according to the customers' preferences.

TudongLawa is a Malaysian company that produces women's hijabs and is part of the country's fashion sector. TudongLawa is a brand-new online store that updates and sells its goods mostly through the Facebook network. This company attempts to provide more customer pleasure while concentrating on offering high-quality hijabs for ladies. To meet the needs of the women, it also provides a variety of hijab kinds and patterns.

The major goal is to market shawl hijabs, which are simple and quick for women to prepare. The longscarf known as a shawl hijab requires a perfect wrap and several pins. Those who wish to get dressed quickly can use any hijab from TudongLawa without bothering about the hijab awning. In actuality, TudongLawa only offered the hijabs in a small number of stocks since it had a restricted number of tailors.

The target market for TudongLawa is any woman, regardless of age, especially those who work and require time to get dressed quickly. Additionally, TudongLawa routinely updates their Facebook page, interacts with clients online, and uses WhatsApp to do so. Customers may order the hijab with a delivery fee of RM8 for West Malaysia and RM14 for East Malaysia by placing an online order. J&T Express or PosLaju Malaysia manage their delivery to other cities. After finishing our social media portfolio, we will boldly work to fulfill our objective and vision to make the TudongLawa brand well-known across Malaysia, which will assist all ladies in wearing the best scarves for their own clothing. When it comes to all of the publishing on their Facebook page, which involves making teaser posts, soft sells, hard sells, and pertinent graphics for each post, we think that our product may attract interest from the community out there.
